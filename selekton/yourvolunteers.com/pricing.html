Free vs. Premium

  1. Home

We do provide our service for free, but we also offer a premium version that provides a few extra features. Most importantly, the premium level removes all advertising.

The term "volunteer focus" has replaced our old "Ongoing Needs" and "Special Events" accounts since we have now combined them in our new version. For example, your organization can have a volunteer focus called, "Office Staff" and then add another one for "Fun Run."
